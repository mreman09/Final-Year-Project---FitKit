Any shoes Anywhere

StepTrack can easily be attached to any shoes allowing you to track how many steps you take a day, a small yet powefull device giving you the data you need.

Improve Fitness

With StepTrack achieve goals more effortly. Have that number of calories you want to lose, let StepTrack know and it will help you maintain a healthy jog by small vibration features allowing you to reach the target you need.

Pairs with your phone

Using the StepTrack app you can view your daily fitness, set step goal and track where you have been. StepTrack will recommend routes to go in order to acheieve step goals
